Here are some quick tips about how to use the d4-client for custom-type applications:

Unzip the archive, move to its location and edit the metaheader.json file:

ja3jl= b'{"type": "ja3-jl", "encoding": "utf-8", "tags": ["tlp:white"]}'

By default, the config generator creates a boilerplate for D4's passiveSSL, by you change it to match your application needs.

Then launch your application and pipe its outputs to d4, for instance for passiveSSL:

{%- if d4_client == 'go' -%} #./d4-tlsf-amd64l -i eth0 | {{"./d4-goclient_%s_%s"%(os_client, arch)}} -c configs {%- endif %}
